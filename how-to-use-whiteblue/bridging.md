# Bridging

### How to Bridge with WhiteBlue

**Step 1:** Go to [whiteblue.to/bridge](https://whiteblue.to/bridge)

**Step 2:** Connect your wallet&#x20;

**Step 3:** Enter transfer details such as: Origin chain, destination chain, token and amount

**Step 4:** Confirm transfer details and click "Send"

**Step 5:** Approve the transaction in your wallet

**Step 6:** Verify transaction details, or monitor its progress, at [whiteblue.to/transactions](https://whiteblue.to/transactions)
